# Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify profitable product categories and strong-performing regions. The goal is to generate insights that support business growth and decision-making.

## Dataset Description
- 9,994 records and 11 columns
- Includes: Sales, Profit, Quantity, Product Category, Sub-Category, Region
- No missing values, already cleaned

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- VS Code

## Key Analysis Performed
1. Overall business performance (total sales and profit)
2. Category-level analysis (sales, profit, profit margin)
3. Regional performance analysis (profit by category across regions)
4. Simple visualizations (bar charts)

## Key Insights
- Snacks is the most efficient category (highest profit margin)
- Oil & Masala has the lowest profit margin
- Snacks performs strongest in Central and South regions relative to other categories
- West region generates the highest total profit from Snacks
- North region has no recorded Snacks data (potential expansion opportunity)

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on Snacks due to high profit efficiency
- Review cost structure and pricing for Oil & Masala
- Explore expansion of Snacks into North region
